About Profitolio

Profitolio is a cryptocurrency portfolio manager designed to help you track your tradings and investment performances. Profitolio allows you to track the market value of your investments along with the volume-weighted average price of individual crypto you have invested. You can keep a journal of buying and selling, and see the current profitability of the portfolio. Realised / Unrealised P&L information are also available for individual cryptocurrency as well as the portfolio wise.
